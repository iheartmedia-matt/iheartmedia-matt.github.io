What We Do

Radio

With iHeartRadio and over 850 radio stations we reach more people every month than any media company in the U.S.

Digital

With our digital network spanning the web, mobile, TV, wearables and auto - we are everywhere our consumers are.

Events

iHeartMedia hosts over 20,000 world-class live events across the U.S. each year connecting fans & artists.

Outdoor

Clear Channel Outdoor is a leading global advertising company reaching more than half a billion people every month.

Social

With iHeartMedia's local & national personalities we have a huge and connected social audience.

Mobile

With Total Traffic & Weather Network we are able to reach nearly every commuter in the U.S. weekly in the car, or mobile on the go.
